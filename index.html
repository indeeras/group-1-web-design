VALUE AND PROTECT

We will always apply the 'best interests' principle. We treat children, and families with sensitivity, fairness, honesty and equity. We aim to deliver the best and most effective service. When a child leaves us they will be in a better position than when they came. We promote an environment of trust and confidence. We provide a network of services and access points island-wide.
